Our Story

This couple? A story of love, a story of faith,
and a story that merely writes itself.
Dan and Tracey both had similar paths in life,
that in the end,
lead them to the same place.
Wanting to give love one more chance,
Dan and Tracey both looked to God for their answers,
which is when the story of the rest of their lives began.
Many of you know the theme of their wedding,
Batman.
Many of you ask, why?
Tracey is a girly girl, who loves pink, yet, no pink at this wedding?
Batman?
Wedding day, you will hear the phenomenon behind this,
and the reason she is marrying a real superhero,
her superhero,
Dan.
